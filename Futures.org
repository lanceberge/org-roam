#+title: Futures

Futures are an agreement to sell an asset
- Cash-settled

* Pricing

  Futures are priced as S x ( 1 + r x t) - dividends
  S - spot
  r - risk free interest rate (5%)
  t - time to expiration

* Margin

  - Losses and gains are credited/debited daily
